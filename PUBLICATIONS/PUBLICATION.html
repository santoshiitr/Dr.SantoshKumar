  1. Application No. : 202211032975A
    Title of Invention : A method for situation-aware hate speech detection in social media
    Name of Applicant: Graphic Era Hill University, Bhimtal Campus, India & Graphic Era Deemed to be University, Dehradun-India
    Name of Inventors: Ananjay Kumar Singh, Ashwini Kumar, VishuTyagi, Santosh Kumar
    Publication Status : Published on 17/06/2022
    Grant Status : -
    Abstract:The present invention relates to a method for situation-aware hate speech detection in social media communication texts representedas quantum vector in quantum information space. The present invention provides an algorithmic approach for hate speech-basedclassifications in online social media. The present invention representing social media conversational text data as quantum vectors in a larger entangled qbit hyper information space, with sub-hyperspace resonating in similar frequencies. The search space as well as the finite quantum vectors bounded by definite probability are now unconstrained with infinite hyper quantum state space. The quantum operations perform all parallel explorations of entangled states in a single unit of time, thus making an algorithmic computation step of O(n) complexity in O (1) , resulting to an exponential algorithmic computation complexity of O(\[ mn \]) in linear order complexity O(n).
